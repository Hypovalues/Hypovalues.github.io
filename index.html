Hypovalues

What is Hypovalues?

Hypovalues is a modification of SapplyValues* that is formatted with different questions to fit different axes. You will be presented with a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

There are questions in the test.

Why use this version?

I modified the classic political compass by eschewing the common left-right axis and replacing it with a nationalist-globalist axis.

In doing so I hope to encourage people to move away from the broad tribal categories of "left" and "right" and instead focus on actual policy points.

I also tried to present questions that are very simple, not specific to a time or place, and - most importantly - fair and unbiased. I say this because I find that many political compass tests have very long, specific, or loaded questions to try and fit the results into a specific worldview. This aims to be not that.

Your questions / results suck!

If you don't like this test, you can always make your own. It's not hard.
